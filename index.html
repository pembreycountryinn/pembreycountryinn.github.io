Our Story
This is your pub

The Pembrey Country Inn is back — and it belongs to you. This is the people's pub, a place where the Pembrey community comes together over a pint, a catch-up, and a warm welcome. Whether you're a familiar face who's been coming here for years or you're popping in for the very first time, you're one of us.

We're honoured to be carrying this pub forward and we're here to build something special with the community at its heart. This isn't just somewhere to have a drink — it's your local, your meeting place, your home from home.

We can't wait to get to know our neighbours, hear your stories, and make The Pembrey Country Inn everything it should be — yours.
