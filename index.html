Our Story
A proper country pub

The Pembrey Country Inn is a traditional country pub where locals and visitors have gathered for years. Whether you're popping in for a quiet pint, settling in for a Sunday roast (more on that coming soon), or enjoying the beer garden on a summer's evening, there's always a warm welcome waiting.

We serve proper pub food (Coming soon!) made with locally sourced ingredients, alongside a well-kept selection of cask ales, craft beers, wines, and spirits.

🍽

Food & Dining (Coming Soon)

From hearty pub classics to seasonal specials, our kitchen serves food that brings people together. We use fresh, locally sourced ingredients wherever possible. Sunday roasts are a house favourite — booking recommended.

🌿

Beer Garden

Our beer garden is the perfect spot to unwind with a cold drink on a warm afternoon. Tucked away with countryside views, it's family-friendly and dog-friendly too. There's nothing better than a pint in the sunshine.
